## üìç **Project Overview**

Business Context/Problem: 
To get insights on which strategies deliver the highest returns, we assess the efficiency of the channels, product profitability, audience response, and seasonal trends.

* Which channels generate the most revenue and ROI?
* Which channels are the most efficient?
* Which products are worth investing more in?

## üìä **Key Findings**

### **Product Performance**

* **Product C** is the top performer ‚Äî highest revenue and highest ROI.
* Product D underperforms consistently across channels and regions.

### **Channel Insights**

* **Email** and **Social Media** generate the most revenue.
* **Direct Mail** has the strongest conversion efficiency but limited reach.
* **Email** has poor conversion efficiency, despite high revenue.

### **Regional Insights**

* **East and West** regions deliver the best ROI.
* North and South consistently underperform.

### **Seasonality**

* **Q3** is the strongest quarter (highest revenue + highest ROI).
* **Q2** is the weak quarter and is ideal for experimentation or testing.

### **Funnel Analysis**

* Strong impressions and clicks, but a major drop-off between clicks and conversions.
* This points to issues with landing pages, user experience, or offer relevance.

---

## üí° **Recommendations**

* Increase investment in **Product C**, especially in East and West regions.
* Improve Email conversion rate through better segmentation and A/B testing.
* Expand Direct Mail campaigns for high-value customers.
* Use Q2 as a test period before heavy spending in Q3.
* Focus on conversion rate optimization to reduce friction after clicks.
